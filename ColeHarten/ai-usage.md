AI tools were used to assist with implementation and development tasks in this project. These tools were also used for minor support tasks such as clarifying APIs and improving code structure.

The overall system architecture, design decisions, and research direction—including the design of Mux and its key mechanisms—were conceived and developed independently by me.

All AI-assisted outputs were reviewed, modified as needed, and validated to ensure correctness and alignment with the intended system design and experimental goals.
